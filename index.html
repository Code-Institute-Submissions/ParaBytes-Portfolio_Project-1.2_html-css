Our Services

Physical Penetration Testing

  • Facility Access Testing
    Evaluate the physical security measures in place by attempting to gain unauthorized access to the premises, including buildings, offices, and restricted areas.
  • Social Engineering Assessments
    Test the effectiveness of security awareness training and policies by conducting simulated social engineering attacks, such as phishing, impersonation, or tailgating.
  • Physical Device Testing
    Assess the security of physical devices, such as servers, workstations, access control systems, or surveillance cameras, to identify vulnerabilities that could be exploited.
  • Red Team Exercises
    Conduct comprehensive simulations of real-world attacks, combining physical and digital techniques, to assess the overall effectiveness of an organization's security posture and incident response capabilities.

Virtual Penetration Testing

  • Web & Mobile App Testing
    Evaluate the security of your organization's web and mobile applications, including identifying vulnerabilities such as injection flaws, cross-site scripting (XSS), and insecure authentication mechanisms, to ensure the protection of user data and prevent unauthorized access.
  • API Penetration Testing
    Assess the security of your organization's APIs by simulating attacks and attempting to exploit vulnerabilities, ensuring that sensitive data and functionality are adequately protected.
  • Cloud Security Assessment
    Evaluate the security of your organization's cloud environment, including access controls, data protection, and configuration settings, to identify potential weaknesses that could be exploited by attackers.
  • Network & Infrastructure Testing
    Assess the security of your organization's network infrastructure, including routers, switches, firewalls, and other network devices, to identify vulnerabilities and potential entry points for attackers.
